About me

I am a Colombian Product Design Engineer with a holistic approach to the digital product lifecycle—from high-level conceptualization and architectural strategy to final production and lifecycle management. Taking into account my team, project, customers and users goals and needs to create surprising and inspiring digital experiences.

Things I like to do

UX Strategy & Research: A good User Experience starts with order, intention and clarity; these characteristics are given to a design through a deep understanding of the user needs, the use cases and flows, the information architecture, and the project goals and limitations. I like to balance all these things to bring out the best possible solution for the users and the market.

UI Architecture: Once I have a good map and understanding of every stakeholder role and needs in the product interaction, I like to sketch and wireframe some quick ideas and validate with users and stakeholders. Once I have a clear idea, I build a comprehensive, responsive and beautiful component library that can be implemented in different instances of the product.

Prototyping & Front-End: Right now I would consider myself a kind of amateur vibe coder, trying to put together good functional HTML CSS Javascript prototypes that are fully functional and interactive, showcasing possible animations and even facilitating the handover to the developers. I like to use Google Antigravity or Figma Make for these kind of feats.

Creative Direction: Through my brands Bonobo and JHOY labs, I explore the boundaries of branding, 3D experimental art, and motion graphics. From cohesive visual languages to Zbrush modeling and Unreal Engine environments.
