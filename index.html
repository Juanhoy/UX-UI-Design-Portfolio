About me

I am a Colombian Product Design Engineer with a holistic approach to the digital product lifecycle—from high-level conceptualization and architectural strategy to final production and lifecycle management. Taking into account my team, project, customers and users goals and needs to create surprising and inspiring digital experiences.


With over 7 years as a Senior UX/UI Designer at Orbit and nearly a decade as the Creative Director of Bonobo, I bridge the gap between rigorous engineering logic and disruptive visual storytelling.

Core Competencies & Technical Stack

UX Strategy & Research: Expert implementation of User Personas, Stakeholder Interviews, and Usability Testing. I utilize a "Double Diamond" approach to ensure problem-solution fit before high-fidelity execution.

UI Architecture: I like to design keeping in mind Atomic Design principles and the development of scalable Design Systems. My workflow is centered in Figma, complemented by Google Stitch for seamless component integration.

Prototyping & Front-End: High-fidelity interaction design and rapid prototyping using Google Antigravity, Claude for logic-driven workflows, and Figma Make.

Creative Direction: Through my brands Bonobo and JHOY, I explore the boundaries of branding, 3D environment design, character illustration, and motion graphics.
